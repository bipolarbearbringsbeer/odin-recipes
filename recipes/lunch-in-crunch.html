Lunch in a Crunch

Description

When you're short on time but need a satisfying and nutritious lunch, this Tuna Salad Sandwich is the perfect solution. Made with flaky tuna, creamy mayo, crunchy celery, and zesty lemon juice, it's a classic that never disappoints.

Ingredients

  • 2 (5 oz) cans tuna, drained
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons diced celery
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 8 slices bread
  • Lettuce and tomato (optional)

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, combine the tuna, mayonnaise, celery, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  2. Mix well until all ingredients are evenly distributed.
  3. Toast the bread slices, if desired.
  4. Spread the tuna salad evenly on 4 of the bread slices.
  5. Top with lettuce and tomato, if using.
  6. Place the remaining 4 bread slices on top to form sandwiches.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
