Dinner Delights

Description

This Creamy Garlic Chicken is a restaurant-quality dish that you can easily make at home. Tender chicken breasts are simmered in a rich, creamy garlic sauce that's sure to impress your family and friends.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked pasta or rice, for serving

Steps

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the butter and olive oil over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the chicken breasts and cook until golden brown on both sides, about 3-4 minutes per side.
  4. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  5. Add the garlic to the skillet and cook for 1 minute, stirring constantly.
  6. Pour in the chicken broth and heavy cream, and whisk to combine.
  7. Return the chicken to the skillet and bring the sauce to a simmer.
  8. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.
  9. Stir in the dried parsley and adjust seasoning if needed.
  10. Serve the chicken and creamy garlic sauce over cooked pasta or rice.
