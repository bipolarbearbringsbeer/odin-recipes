Breakfast Bliss

Description

This Breakfast Burrito is a delicious and satisfying way to start your day. Filled with scrambled eggs, chorizo, potatoes, cheese, and your choice of toppings, it's a meal that will keep you energized until lunch.

Ingredients

  • 6 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup chorizo, cooked and crumbled
  • 1 cup diced potatoes
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Toppings (salsa, avocado, sour cream, etc.)

Steps

  1. Cook the chorizo and potatoes according to package instructions. Set aside.
  2. Scramble the eggs in a pan, seasoning with salt and pepper.
  3. Warm the tortillas to make them pliable.
  4. Place a quarter of the scrambled eggs, chorizo, potatoes, and cheese in the center of each tortilla.
  5. Fold the sides of the tortilla over the filling and roll up tightly.
  6. Serve warm with desired toppings.
